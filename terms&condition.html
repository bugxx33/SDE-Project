TERMS AND CONDITION

(SCEA)

Eligibility: The student must be a bonafide student of the University.

Non-member renter: For students who are not from the College of Engineering and Architecture and have yet to pay the membership fee, a rental fee will be applicable. The students must pay rental fees in full before renting any item(s).

Timely return: The student is responsible for personally returning the rented item(s) immediately after use to ensure their availability for other users. Upon returning the item must be in the same condition.

Damage responsibility: If a rented item is damaged, the student is responsible for repairing and returning requirements will result in the student blacklisted from the council services and will be on hold upon the signing of clearance.

Loss Responsibility: In the event of loss, both the item and its accessories must be replaced or paid for before the end of the semester. Failure to comply with the requirements will result in the student being blacklisted from the council services and will be on hold upon the signing of clearance. 

Custodian Responsibility: In case of unforeseen events, the custodian in charge will be held responsible for any loss and damage. Identification and Contact: The students shall surrender any valid ID upon renting. The student must provide a valid ID and contact details for tracking purposes.

Releasing, Returning and Extension: Releasing and returning items is within office hours from 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM. Once released,the return of the rented item shall have a maximum of 3 days. Failure to do so will result in a penalty fee. Extension requests shall be provided through letter and will be reviewed and approved based on availability and necessity. 

Rights to Deny: The Student Council of Engineering and Architecture reserves the right to deny a service request.




(UCS Terms of Borrowing)

The Borrower shall return the borrowed items on or before the agreed - upon date.
The Borrower agrees to take proper care of the borrowed items and return them in the same condition as when they were received.
If the borrowed items are damaged or lost while in the Borrower’s possession, the Borrower shall be responsible for the repair or replacement.
If the borrowed item is not returned on time, there will be a 5 pesos penalty per hour or 20 pesos per day.
